2023 - A Quick Bite

Going home with freinds always makes the ride feel safer, and on a hot day back to school an over priced pepsi float helps us cool down.

As a kid when I would go to the park I always got a sweet treat from the Mr.Frosty truck that would pull up to its iconic jingle.

This is an oil painting that commemorates the end of my day going home with friends.

(12x18) oil on canvas 2023
